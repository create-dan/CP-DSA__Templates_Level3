- FIFO --> first in First Out
- Elements are inserted at the back (end) and are deleted from the front.
- Use of queue is in cpu scheduling and process scheduling
- front=pop , rear = push
- rear==n-1 , queue full 
- front==rear , queue empty

Time Compexity-
isEmpty,push,pop,size = o(1) (front==rear)

- Input restricted queue
- Output restricted queue
- Doubly Ended queue

-  Double-ended queues are a special case of queues where insertion and deletion operations are possible at both the ends.
